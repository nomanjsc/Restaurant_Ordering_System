User Manual & Guide

A complete step-by-step guide to using the Busan Bites skiok. Follow the instructions below to place your order.

Step 1

Browsing & Adding Items

The main screen displays all available menu items with their prices in Won (₩).

  • Burger: ₩5000
  • Pizza: ₩8000
  • Fries: ₩3000

Click the Add button on the right side of an item to place it in your cart.

Figure 1: Main Menu Interface

Step 2

Managing Your Cart

The Cart Screen shows a summary of your selected items.

  • Quantity: Use the up/down arrows to order more of the same item.
  • Remove: Click the "Remove" button to delete an item.
Note: You cannot proceed to checkout if the cart is empty.

Figure 2: Cart Summary

Step 3

Checkout & Payment

Review your total and enter your payment details.

Minimum 4 Digits

You must enter a card number with at least 4 digits. The system will show a warning popup (see Figure 3.1) if the number is too short.

After entering the number, click Confirm Order.

Figure 3: Checkout Input

Figure 3.1: Invalid Card Warning

Order Complete!

You will see the confirmation screen above. Your order has been sent to the kitchen. Click "Exit" to close the application or "Back to Menu" to order again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using the kiosk.

Why is my cart empty?

You must add items from the Menu Screen before opening the cart.

Why isn’t my card number accepted?

Minimum 4 digits are required for validation.

How do I remove an item from the cart?

Click the 'Remove' button next to the item.

Can I adjust quantity?

Yes, use the arrow buttons next to the quantity box.

Where can I find the total?

Total is displayed at the bottom of Cart & Checkout screens.

Does the system save my previous cart?

No, the cart resets every time the system restarts.
